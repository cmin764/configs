# Transport Modes Reference

> **Arc colors** are universal. **Cost ranges** are vetted examples for
> Colombia, Ecuador, and Central America (2025-2026). Add a section per
> country/region as new trips are planned. Use general knowledge for
> uncovered regions and update here after verifying on the ground.

## Arc colors and styles (Leaflet map)

| Mode       | Color   | Hex       | Dash pattern | Notes                          |
|------------|---------|-----------|--------------|-------------------------------|
| FLY        | Blue    | #3b82f6   | 8,4 dashed   | All flights                   |
| BUS        | Amber   | #d97706   | solid        | Long-distance coaches          |
| WILLY      | Amber   | #d97706   | 4,3 dotted   | Jeeps / colectivos / shared    |
| BOAT       | Teal    | #0891b2   | solid        | Ferries, water taxis, lancha   |
| TAXI       | Grey    | #6b7280   | solid        | Pre-arranged or app-booked     |
| TRANSFER   | Green   | #16a34a   | solid        | Private pre-booked transfer    |
| METRO      | Purple  | #7c3aed   | solid        | Urban metro / cable car        |
| WALK       | Grey    | #9ca3af   | 2,4 dotted   | Walking legs only              |

Arc label format: `MODE · ~Xkm · Xh` or `MODE · ~Xkm · Xmin`

## Cost ranges by country (2025-2026)

### Colombia
- Domestic flight: $40-120 USD/person (BASIC fare)
- Long-distance bus (4-6h): 25,000-50,000 COP/person
- Short bus (1-2h): 8,000-20,000 COP/person
- Willy jeep: 5,000-15,000 COP/person
- Airport Uber (major cities): 30,000-80,000 COP
- InDrive minimum (Cartagena): 8,000 COP
- Metro (Medellín): ~3,200-3,500 COP/tap

### Ecuador
- Domestic flight (mainland): $60-150 USD
- Galápagos flight (mainland→islands): $180-300 USD/person
- Inter-island ferry: $30-40 USD/person
- Bus (long-distance): $5-15 USD

### Central America (general)
- Shuttle services: $15-40 USD/person per leg
- Water taxi: $5-20 USD/person
- Public bus: $1-5 USD
